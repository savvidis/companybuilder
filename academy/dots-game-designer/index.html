Dots – Game Designer

Apply Now

We’re building more games!  We’re scaling our team and titles (games) in pairs of two.  Each title has a game designer paired with a mobile engineer.  As a game designer, you’ll work with our product team on a new title, likely building everything from game mechanic, design, and user experience.  You’ll be paired with a ridiculously talented and creative mobile engineer to turn your prototypes and concepts into a world class app.

 Responsibilities

  • Work in partnership with product team to conceptualize and prototype new titles

  • Create storyboard, visuals, mockups to communicate concept

  • Work with your mobile engineer to turn a concept in high-quality mobile app

  • Communicate project status to team in an accurate and detailed way

  • Work with peer teams to share best practices and help out as needed

 

Qualifications

  • 2 years experience professionally or personally in game design

  • Familiar with Objective-C; 6 months experience in iOS development preferred

  • Self-reliant leader that can quickly adopt new systems and languages

  • Strong communicator with excellent design skills
