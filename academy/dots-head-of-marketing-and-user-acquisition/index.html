Dots – Head of User Acquisition

Apply Now

From USA Today to CNN to The Guardian, Dots has enjoyed no shortage of attention.  We’ve also had strong success running traditional marketing and user acquisition campaigns with various UA platforms.  We’ve dabbled in more innovative, viral marketing more recently by helping a nice guy propose to his girlfriend with their favorite game, Dots, that was picked up by TV stations and news outlets around the world.  We’re looking for an ultra analytical Head of User Acquisition to drive download growth in partnership during large marketing and press pushes.  The ideal candidate has extensive experience buying (and possibly selling) on CPM, CPC, and CPI basis with numerous UA platforms.

Responsibilities:

  • Test, evaluate, and benchmark new performance advertising channels

  • Manage user acquisition, analytics, processes and budget

  • Deep, expert-level understanding of user acquisition industry trends

  • Provide input on Dots brand marketing and PR

  • Conceptualize and executive creative user acquisition campaigns

  • Collaborate with designers to generate compelling creative

  • Competitive market research and analysis

Qualifications:

  • Minimum 2 years experience in digital marketing with focus on data analytics, campaign optimization, and user acquisition

  • Strong analytical background (finance, statistics, or economics experience)

  • Extensive experience marketing casual games and mobile apps

  • Strong education from reputable institution

  • Fluent in written and spoken English; additional languages a plus

  • Create marketing operations infrastructure and process to support rapid growth

  • Deep understanding of marketing and user acquisition industry trends

  • Strike balance of strong individual contributor and team leader
