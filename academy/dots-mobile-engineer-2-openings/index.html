Dots – Mobile Engineer (2 openings)

Apply Now

We’re building more games!  We’re scaling our team and titles (games) in pairs of two.  Each title has a game designer paired with a mobile engineer.  Our game designers know how to code and prototype, but are often self-taught and spend more time thinking about game mechanic, design, and user experience, so they work best when paired with a ridiculously talented and creative mobile engineer.  You’ll enjoy working with our team on the entire design process and get to focus on what you do best — building apps.

 

Responsibilities

  • Work with your game designer to turn a concept in high-quality mobile app

  • Write beautiful self-documenting code that others can easily read and adapt

  • Participate in regular code reviews

  • Communicate iOS project status to team in an accurate and detailed way

  • Work with peer teams to share best practices and help out as needed

 

Qualifications

  • 2 years experience in iOS development

  • 6 months experience in Cocos2D development

  • Proficient in C and Objective-C

  • Self-reliant hacker that can quickly adopt new systems and languages

  • Strong communicator with excellent problem solving skills
