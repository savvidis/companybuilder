Dots – Head of Engineering

Apply Now

We’re adding millions of new players to Dots each month.  With this scale comes new and interesting challenges on how to efficiently organize and process profile and leaderboard data.  In addition to data, we’re building a creative and talented engineering team in need of a strong technical leader.  Our ideal candidate is as creative as they are process-driven, and knows how to get the best out of a strong team.  We’re not stuck to any specific technologies, but we’re currently using Cocos2D, PHP, MySQL, Apportable SDK (for Android), with our API scaled on multiple Amazon EC2 machines.

Responsibilities:

  • Provide strategic direction of technical, architectural and design efforts
  • Build and organize growing team with technical disciplines
  • Manage delivery of multiple and concurrent product releases
  • Build, maintain, and evangelize technology best practices across organization
  • Create dev operations infrastructure and process to support rapid growth
  • Deep understanding of industry trends and emerging technologies
  • Work with product team to build a strong quality assurance practice that includes manual and automated testing
  • Personally own a specific technical function as an active developer (iOS, infrastructure, or API)

Qualifications:

  • 3 years of technical management and development experience
  • Desire and passion for pushing the bounds of latest technologies
  • Experience with Agile/Scrum/Lean principles and values
  • Experience building scalable solutions for handling high volume traffic
  • Strong communication, analytical and presentation skills
  • Deep subject matter expertise of a specific technical function (infrastructure, backend development, frontend web or mobile development, etc)
