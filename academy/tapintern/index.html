Tapestry – Internship

Apply Now

Tapestry is working to change the world of storytelling and we’d really like you to join us on this adventure.

We’re looking to grow our community of writers and readers quickly, and you’ll be on our front lines helping us make that happen. So what exactly will you be doing if you come on board?

  • Create original Tapestry stories to help us grow our quality library (this includes writing, illustrating, and just straight up being creative) Basic photoshop a must, and HTML/CSS a major plus.

  • Help us push the envelope on social networks to give our community and their stories the reach they deserve

  • Be our go to for community support by answering emails, twitter questions, facebook replies, tumblr love, texts, smoke signals–whatever it takes to make our community feel awesome.

  • Be on our Tapestry Education Committee. More details will come but we’re looking to you to help us build out ideas for Tapestry product to support our higher ed community. Come with ideas on how you see Tapestry fitting into college writing courses.

  • This bullet point is reserved for just about anything else that will come up on a day to day basis at a startup. It’s gonna get hectic but we’ll make sure you’re learning AND having fun.

By joining our internship program you’ll have an opportunity to not only do some really cool cutting edge work for Tapestry, but you’ll have an amazing opportunity to learn along the way.

  • Tapestry works out of the betaworks studio in the Meatpacking District in Manhattan, right next to The High Line and Chelsea Market.

  • betaworks studios houses over 10 other early stage companies. It is an extremely fun and fast paced environment (we’re not just saying that, you’ll need your rest to keep up with us:)

  • Partake in the betaworks summer intern program (weekly meetings with all of the betaworks company’s interns, New York City outings, special speakers just for you, oh my!)

  • You’ll get to attend the betaworks weekly town hall meetings with company demos of new features and products that are being launched constantly. You will get the chance to hear about and be a part of new technology companies very early on.

  • You will work side by side with in-house talent and mentors who work out of the betaworks studio office (such as the VP of User Experience, GM’s and CEO’s that lead the industry in user acquisition, community building…we can go on and on here).

  • We have connections with leading companies that meet with us to share knowledge on analytics, Facebook integration, virality, lean design, and more.

  • betaworks holds lunch and learn presentations with speakers such as NASA Astronaut Ron Garan, Friends of The High Line, Data Scientists, Artists and more.

  • You will get access to the newest professional tools from Adobe, Facebook, Chartbeat, Google Analytics, and other companies, which you can learn and use while completing your internship projects.

  • Tapestry and all betaworks companies are moving very quickly, growing our teams, launching new products – so you will be in a fast paced environment where your work is creating an immediate impact and you are developing a real portfolio.

Come prepared to fight fires, walk on water, and be part of an extremely dynamic, fast-paced work environment where everyone’s contributions are respected and appreciated.
