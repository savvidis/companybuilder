Purpose

We are not venture capitalists and we are not consultants. First and foremost we are builders of new ventures.

Our ideas, people, knowledge and data are united in an imaginative way that enable us to create beneficial and transformative solutions for companies, which want to go beyond their comfort level to explore new opportunities. We set-up new ventures and build a strong network of people along the way.

 

Projects
