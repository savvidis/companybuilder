This Privacy Policy covers the treatment of personal information gathered when you are using or accessing the following services we offer (the “Service”):

While we attempt to facilitate access only to those third party services that share our respect for your privacy, we cannot take responsibility for the content or privacy policies of such third party services. We encourage you to carefully review the privacy policies of any third party services you access.

You can unsubscribe from receiving Openbeta emails at any time.  You can unsubscribe by clicking on the “Unsubscribe” link in the footer of any Openbeta email and following the instructions.  You can also unsubscribe by clicking on the “Unsubscribe” link on the bottom of the Openbeta homepage and following the instructions.

If you’ve been subscribing to our emails then from time to time you’ve also been receiving emails marked “Allied Info” in the subject line and in the email header.  These emails are content paid for by our sponsors (allies).

You can unsubscribe from receiving Allied Info emails.  However, if you do so, you will no longer receive Openbeta emails either.  You can unsubscribe by clicking on the “Unsubscribe” link in the footer of any Openbeta email and following the instructions.  You can also unsubscribe by clicking on the “Unsubscribe” link on the bottom of the Openbeta homepage and following the instructions.
